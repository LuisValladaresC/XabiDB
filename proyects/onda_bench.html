BILBAO

Bilbao is a city full of contrasts, being mixture between industry and modern architecture a very interesting point.

COOL TOUCH

Station users have determined that its cool touch is the most negative aspect of the current benches.

SEQURITY

The bench is going to be placed in the Indalecio Prieto train station, a semi-opened station with 24h security.

Onda shapes, which are not usual for station benchs, are a mixture of Bilbao's modern architecture.

The seats are made of wood in order to solve its cool touch problem, which is also strengthened by showing the grain of the wood.

An amount of 50 interviews were made in order to discover the main weaknesses of the current bench.

Where you can see that most of the people complained about bench's cool touch followed by its uncomfortability, which also could be consequence of the cool touch.
