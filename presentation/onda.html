Briefing

The objective of this project was to design a kit with the necessary material to implement and maintain an edible balcony.

Positioning

After analysing the market, it has been determined that the new product will be an aromatic plant growing kit which will be simple, didactic and eco-friendly.

The logotype gathers some of the products properties. Two different fonts are used for the brand whose name also has two different meanings. The decorative element is very simple and gives to the C a resemblance to a fruit.
